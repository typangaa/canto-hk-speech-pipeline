## 7. KEY FINDINGS

### What worked:
- Web searches returned many useful results for beauty vloggers, cooking channels, and Cantonese podcasts
- YouTube channel verifications were successful for 3 out of 4 channels
- Apple Podcasts and Spotify have rich Cantonese female-hosted content
- Flo女人同學會 and SistersTalkTalkShow are particularly strong finds with high episode counts

### What did not work:
- @LizzyDaily returned 404 - channel appears to be defunct or renamed
- Firstory.me search function returned 404 - platform lacks a reliable web search
- Some search queries returned too broad results (general Chinese content, not specifically HK Cantonese female-hosted)

### Gaps identified:
- Fewer dedicated HK Cantonese female cooking channels compared to beauty
- Many beauty vloggers are more Taiwan-focused than HK-specific
- Comedy podcasts with female hosts are limited; true crime/chat dominates
- More searching needed for parenting/motherhood specific podcasts
